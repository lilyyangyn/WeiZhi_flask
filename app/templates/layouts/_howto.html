How To Order

1. Register

Go to Register page. Use your HKU email to register and activate.

2. Order

Login and order from 9:00—11:00 a.m. Become VIP to order 12 hours earlier, i.e. 9:00 p.m.—11:00 a.m.

3. Pickup

Get your takeout from 12:20—12:40 near the pillar outside SU canteen.
